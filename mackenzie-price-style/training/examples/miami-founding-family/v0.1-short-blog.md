# Alpha Miami Founding Families, We’re Almost There

Starting a new school is a big moment for kids, but I think parents feel it just as much.

I remember wondering if my own kids would feel known, make friends, be challenged, and come home still excited about learning. Those questions are exactly why the first Alpha Miami founding family meetup matters.

Before the school year begins, our founding families will come together to meet each other, connect with the Alpha Miami team, and help our students walk into day one already feeling part of something.

This first group of families will help shape the culture of Alpha Miami from the very beginning.

We are so excited to get started.
