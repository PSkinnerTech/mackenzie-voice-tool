## What to review

1. One email draft to founding families.
2. One short blog draft to founding families.

## How to collect feedback

For each artifact, ask MacKenzie:

1. What sounds like me?
2. What absolutely does not sound like me?
3. What would you say instead?
4. Which phrases should we never use again?
5. Which phrases should we reuse?
6. Is anything factually wrong or off-brand for Alpha?
7. Score 1-5: sounds like me, clarity, parent resonance, not generic.

## Capture format

Save notes to `training/examples/feedback-ledger.jsonl` with:

- artifact_id
- draft_path
- reviewer
- scores
- forbidden_phrases
- keeper_phrases
- reviewer_notes_verbatim
- accepted_revision
- timestamp
